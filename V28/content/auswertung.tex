\section{Evaluation}
\label{sec:Evaluation}
To determine the g-factor, formula \eqref{eq:hnu} can be converted to
\begin{equation}
  g = \frac{h \nu}{\mu_{\mathrm{B}} B},
  \label{eqn:g}
\end{equation}
with the Planck constan $h = \SI{6.626e-34}{\joule\second}$ \cite{h} and the Bohr magneton $\mu_{\mathrm{B}} = \SI{927.4e-26}{\joule\per\tesla}$ \cite{mub}.
The used frequencies and currents can be taken from table \ref{tab:g-factor}, also the calculated values for the magnetic field strength by equation \eqref{eq:hh}.
